Colonial Volunteers

The Colonial Heritage Festival is the largest American Colonial life and re-enactment event in the western United States. With exhibits from more than 100 artisans, educators, and re-enactors, the event attracts more than 50,000 visitors annually (see http://festival.colonialheritage.org). The Colonial Heritage Foundation relies heavily on volunteers for the event’s staffing needs. People who have an interest in helping are assigned to specific areas in one of three shifts on each day of the event. The workbook that forms the foundation of this assignment is used for updating volunteer information and for assigning volunteers to shifts for the event. In this assignment, you will add code to an existing user form make it easier for a user to update volunteer information and to provide data validation to ensure the quality of the data entered. Note: Do not use the VBA statement "End" in any part of your code. This statement will interrupt the grader and prevent your submission from being completed.
